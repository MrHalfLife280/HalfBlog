The main problems with YouTube Revivals

March 30, 2025 MrHalfLife

Hello,

So, as you know, I used to have a BitView account, and currently own a KamTape and EraCast account. My BitView account got banned, if you wanna read the reason, look at the post "About the whole BitView stuff".

The problem is that the moderation of most of the Revivals sucks, you can find music videos on BitView from big artists like Madonna (which I reported), I like the way that EraCast handles Copyright, they have a Content-ID-like feature where if you upload copyrighted material, it will be auto-flagged.

Another problem is the UTTP, there will be these disgusting pieces of crap on these sites and so.

But the mods are really nice, and I have met multiple nice people on these revivals, like A Treeager.

And that's all I have to say about that
